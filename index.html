Business Law

Encompasses the formation of new businesses and any issues that arise during the course of the business's life.

Criminal Law

It prescribes conduct perceived as threatening, harmful, health, safety, and moral welfare of people inclusive of one's self.

Child Support

Deals with the legal obligation of non-custodial parents to contribute financially to the rearing of their children.

Education Law

A set of rules and regulations that guide the workings of an education system, that focuses on laws and regulations that govern federal and state schools and school boards and school systems.

Divorce Law

It is the legal process of ending a marriage, The complete guidance of divorce procedure in Bangladesh, which includes the procedure for Muslim, Hundu & Chrisitan divorce under Bangladesh Family Law.

Tax Law

Body of rules under which a public authority has a claim on taxpayers, requiring them to transfer to the authority part of their income or property, which impose taxes is generally recognized as a right of governments.
